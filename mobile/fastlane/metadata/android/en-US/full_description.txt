FlupFlap is a marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of cultural products from African, Caribbean, Asian, and other global communities.

Browse handcrafted goods, traditional clothing, foods, art, beauty products, and more — sourced from independent sellers who are passionate about their culture.

**For buyers:**
- Discover unique products from around the world
- Filter by category, seller location, and price
- Secure checkout via Stripe
- Track your orders in the app

**For sellers:**
- Create a seller profile and list your products in minutes
- Manage inventory, orders, and shipping from one place
- Receive payouts directly through Stripe Connect
- Reach customers who value authentic cultural goods

FlupFlap is committed to celebrating cultural diversity through commerce. All sellers agree to our conduct policy, and prohibited items are not permitted on the platform.

For support, visit flupflap.com or contact us through the app.
