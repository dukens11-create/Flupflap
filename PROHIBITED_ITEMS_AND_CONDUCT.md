# FlupFlap Prohibited Items & Conduct Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-07

FlupFlap is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and lawful marketplace for all users. To protect our community, certain items, content, and conduct are strictly prohibited on the platform.

By using FlupFlap, you agree not to post, list, upload, sell, promote, share, or distribute any prohibited item or content described below.

## 1. Hate, discrimination, and violent extremism

FlupFlap does not allow content or items that:

- promote, support, glorify, or encourage hatred, discrimination, or violence against individuals or groups;
- target people based on race, color, ethnicity, national origin, nationality, religion, caste, immigration status, disability, medical condition, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, or other protected characteristics;
- include hate symbols, extremist propaganda, or materials intended to intimidate, humiliate, dehumanize, or degrade others;
- praise, support, or promote terrorist, extremist, or violent hate organizations;
- encourage or threaten physical harm against any person or group.

## 2. Harassment, threats, and abusive behavior

Users may not use FlupFlap to post, send, or promote:

- threats of violence or bodily harm;
- harassment, stalking, intimidation, bullying, or targeted abuse;
- obscene, degrading, or abusive content directed toward another person;
- content intended to exploit, shame, humiliate, or endanger others.

## 3. Illegal, dangerous, or fraudulent items

Users may not list, sell, or distribute:

- illegal goods or services;
- stolen goods;
- counterfeit, pirated, or otherwise infringing items;
- weapons, explosives, or hazardous materials where prohibited by law;
- illegal drugs, controlled substances, or prohibited paraphernalia;
- fraudulent, deceptive, misleading, or scam-related listings.

## 4. Adult, exploitative, or abusive content

FlupFlap strictly prohibits:

- sexually exploitative content;
- non-consensual sexual content or imagery;
- child sexual abuse material or any exploitative content involving minors;
- items, services, or content that facilitate abuse, exploitation, or trafficking.

## 5. Misrepresentation and platform abuse

Users may not:

- impersonate another person, brand, or organization;
- provide false identity, payment, or listing information;
- manipulate reviews, transactions, moderation systems, or other platform features;
- engage in scams, fraudulent disputes, bad-faith chargebacks, or deceptive conduct.

## 6. Enforcement

FlupFlap reserves the right to investigate and take action against any listing, content, message, or account that violates this policy or otherwise creates risk for users or the platform.

Enforcement actions may include:

- removal of listings or content;
- warnings;
- temporary restrictions;
- seller suspension;
- permanent account termination;
- reporting to law enforcement or regulatory authorities when appropriate.

## 7. Reporting violations

If you believe a listing, user, or message violates this policy, please report it through the platform or contact FlupFlap support.

## 8. Policy updates

FlupFlap may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
